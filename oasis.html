Oasia Hotel


An automatic irrigation system was designed to
irrigate planter boxes and garden beds on a 28
storey hotel next to the Tanjong Pagar MRT. The
irrigation system has two water supplies via a
pumping system to levels 27 & 28 and a gravity
feed supply feeding the lower levels.

Client: Tropical Environment.
