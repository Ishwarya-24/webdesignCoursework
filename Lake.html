Lake & Pond Management


Keeping nutrients at a minimal level is a prime requirement in an eco-balance water body. Aerobic digestion is a key element in the reduction of nutrients and keeping the water clean and healthy.


FLOATING ISLANDS
Floating islands create a wetlands effect within the water body. That is, a life support for microbes that eat nutrients. Circulation and surface area is required to support the maximum volume of water past the microbes. In addition, a floating island provides food for fish plus a safe haven for fish and birds.
AERATION
Dissolved Oxygen in the water creates a platform of aerobic digestion and aquatic animal life support. Bacteria which digest nutrients require oxygen to survive. This is a range of aeration products that offer aesthetic appeal while adding oxygen and breaking down stratification.
