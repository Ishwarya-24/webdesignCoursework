Eagle Farm Race Track - Brisbane


Evergreen constructed a 27m wide track proper
and a 10m wide “A” grass track. Toro valve in
heads sprinklers we selected and controller by a
Rain Bird Site Control central computer system.
Submersible pumps suspended under floats supply
the water for a large dam.

Client: Brisbane Turf Club.
Main Con: Evergreen Turf.
